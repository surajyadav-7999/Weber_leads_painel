Weberleads.in

Step One

  • 1. You have To Login First on Weberleads.in
  • 2. Go To Compose SMS then go to English SMS
  • 3. YOU have To Create Sender Id Click on Plush Button
  • 4. Click on Create Sender ID Button on Pop Up Box you will be Display
  • 5. Put your sender id which is already approved on dlt platform
  • 6. Put your Entity-ID which is 19 digit
  • 7. Put your SMS CONTENT which is approved on dlt platform without any mistik
  • 8. Click on Save Button

Sconde Step

After Completion of above step

  • 1. you have to create your Templates Click on Pluse button after cliking one Pop Up Box you will be Display
  • 2. You have to put your Templates which is already approved on dlt platform
  • 3. You have to put your Template-ID which is 19 digit
  • 4. Put your SMS CONTENT which is approved on dlt platform without any mistik
  • 5. Send SMS Now

http://weberleads.in/http-tokenkeyapi.php?authentic-key=363138384f545036303416 &senderid=6char-senderid&route=2&number= enternumber& templateid:enternumerictemplateid& message=hellothere

http://weberleads.in/http-jsonapi.php ?username=enterusername& password=enterpassword& senderid=6char-senderid& route=2&number=enternumber& templateid:enternumerictemplateid& message=hello there

Hellotext.live

Step One

  • 1. You have To Login First on hellotext.live
  • 2. Go To Compose SMS then go to Quick Send SMS
  • 3. YOU have To Create Sender Id Click on Plus Button
  • 4. Click on Create Sender ID Button on Pop Up Box you will be Display
  • 5. Put your sender id which is already approved on dlt platform
  • 6. Put your PE-ID which is 19 digit
  • 7. Put your SMS CONTENT which is approved on dlt platform without any mistik

Sconde Step

After Completion of above step

  • 1. Go to Manager Click on Manage Template
  • 2. On top right corner click on Create Template button
  • 3. You have to put your Template Name which already approved from dlt platform
  • 4. You have to Select Type* of Message (English/Unicode)
  • 4. You have to put your Template ID*
  • 6. Put your SMS CONTENT which is approved on dlt platform without any mistik
  • 5. Click On submit Button
